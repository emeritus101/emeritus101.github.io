My town, Charlotte

General Overview


Charlotte is the biggest city in North Carolina.

Situated in the Piedmonth region, Charlotte lies at the Southern border with South Carolina. There are over 800,000 individuals living in the city and over 2,000,000 in the Metropolitan area sorounding it

Founded in the sixteenth century, it is strategically positioned only 3 hours away from The Atlantic Ocean and about the same from The Blue Ridge Mountains. For many years a religious center for North America, it is today one of the most important financial centers of the country.
